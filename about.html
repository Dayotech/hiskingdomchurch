About HIS KINGDOM MINISTRY

Built on the Word of God, prayer, holiness, and sound biblical teaching.

Who We Are

HIS KINGDOM MINISTRY is a Bible-centered ministry committed to teaching the truth of God’s Word without compromise. Our focus is to lead people to Christ, strengthen believers in sound doctrine, and prepare souls for the Kingdom of God.

Our Vision

To raise believers who love the Word of God, live in obedience to Christ, walk in holiness, and prepare for the coming Kingdom of God.

Our Mission

To preach repentance, teach sound doctrine, encourage prayer, promote scripture reading, and guide people into a deeper relationship with Jesus Christ.

Our Biblical Focus

  • Salvation through Jesus Christ
  • Repentance and holy living
  • Sound doctrine and deep Bible teaching
  • Prayer and obedience to God
  • Preparing believers for the coming of Christ

Pastor Semiu Olaniyan

Pastor Semiu Olaniyan is committed to biblical truth, deep scriptural teaching, and the spiritual growth of believers. His ministry burden is to help people know God through His Word and live according to the teachings of Jesus Christ.
