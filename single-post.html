Why Every Believer Must Study the Word of God

Sound Doctrine | Bible Study | Spiritual Growth

Published by Pastor Semiu Olaniyan | HIS KINGDOM MINISTRY

The Word of God Is the Foundation of Christian Life

A believer cannot grow spiritually without the Word of God. The Bible reveals the mind of God, the will of God, the character of God, and the way of salvation through Jesus Christ.

Many people today desire spiritual power, but they neglect spiritual truth. True Christian strength begins when the believer gives attention to scripture, prayer, obedience, and daily meditation on God’s Word.

Why Bible Study Is Important

Bible study helps believers to know God personally, avoid deception, understand doctrine, grow in faith, and live a life that pleases the Lord.

“Study to shew thyself approved unto God...” — 2 Timothy 2:15

How To Study The Bible Properly

  • Pray before reading.
  • Read slowly and carefully.
  • Compare scripture with scripture.
  • Write down lessons and questions.
  • Obey what God reveals through His Word.

Conclusion

The Word of God must not be treated as ordinary writing. It is spiritual food, light, correction, wisdom, and truth. Every believer who wants to stand firm in this generation must return to deep study of the scriptures.
